FAQ About Tea Spill Game

1. What is the Tea Spill Game?

The Tea Spill Game is a viral internet trend inspired by the chaos surrounding the Tea App, a controversial social platform where users were unknowingly ranked, voted on, and leaked. This site recreates that leaderboard-style experience—not by running the original Tea App, but by reflecting the internet meltdown it caused.

2. Is this connected to the Tea App?

No — this website is not affiliated with the original Tea App. However, it was inspired by the “tea spill” moment that followed: leaked user photos, viral leaderboards, and mass outrage on TikTok and Reddit. This page curates what’s already been publicly circulating—purely for meme, commentary, and educational purposes.

3. Is there a Tea App download?

Yes, the Tea App is still available for download on some app stores, though it may appear under different names or descriptions. It became controversial for featuring real people in a ranking and rating system without consent—sparking debate over privacy and internet ethics. This page does not host the app, but it does reflect the viral impact the Tea Spill App had on Gen Z and beyond.
